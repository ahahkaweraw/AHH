Cookie Recipe List because Why Not?

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Sea Salt
  • Light Brown Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Salted Butter
  • Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Baking Powder

Directions

  1. Grab a bowl
  2. Agressively shove all ingredients in the bowl
  3. MIX MIX MIX
  4. Realize this isn't right
  5. Give up

In Depth Directions

Add dry ingredients
Flour, Baking Soda, Baking Powder and a little bit of salt into a bowl then mix lightly

Make Brown Butter
Melt your butter and stir until it looks golden brown then let it cool

Mix in your wet ingredients
Brown Sugar, Granulated Sugar, Pure Vanilla Extract, Brown Butter and mix

COOKIE DOUGH
Add Eggs, Dry Ingredients, and lastly Chocolate Chips to the wet ingredients

Bake
Put cookie dough on to a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 12-14 minutes

EAT!!!
